Why I want to become a developer?

I want to be a developer because I am drawn to the balance of creative problem-solving and tangible impact. I love the process of taking a complex, abstract problem, breaking it down, and engineering a functional solution from scratch.Technology evolves rapidly, and the necessity for continuous learning genuinely excites me. Ultimately, software development allows me to build scalable tools that can directly improve efficiency and solve real-world problems for users every day.
