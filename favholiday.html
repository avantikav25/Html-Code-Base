SEOUL, SOUTH KOREA

Seoul is the vibrant capital of South Korea, known for its modern skyscrapers, rich cultural heritage, delicious food, and exciting nightlife. The city beautifully blends ancient palaces and traditional markets with advanced technology and contemporary attractions, making it one of the most popular tourist destinations in Asia.

Top 5 Attractions:
  1. Gyeongbokgung Palace - Explore the historic royal palace of the Joseon Dynasty.
  2. N Seoul Tower - Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the city.
  3. Bukchon Hanok Village - Experience traditional Korean architecture and culture.
  4. Myeongdong - Visit the famous shopping and street food district.
  5. Han River Park - Relax by the beautiful river and enjoy outdoor activities.
Must-Try Foods:
  • Kimchi - A traditional Korean side dish made from fermented vegetables.
  • Bibimbap - A popular rice dish mixed with vegetables, meat, and egg.
  • Tteokbokki - A spicy and delicious Korean street food.
  • Korean BBQ - Enjoy grilled meat cooked at your table.
  • Hotteok - A sweet Korean pancake filled with sugar and nuts
